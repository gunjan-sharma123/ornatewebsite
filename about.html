About Us

Welcome To Ornate TechnoServices

Ornate TechnoServices started Operation in 2009 as an Offshore Outsourcing proprietorship firm and in 2011 became Private Limited Company. Since than we are focussed to provide the technology services in domain ­ Software Application Development, Mobile Application Development, Web Application Development, E commerce, IT Staffing and Digital Marketing through continuous innovation to create a value propositions for our clients. Ornate TechnoServices resources follows the SDLC process and other standard process of ISO 9001, ISO 20000 & ISO 27001 for in house product development as well for client’s application development, Our laid processes are independent of technology platforms and is our habit to follow the same irrespective whether we are working out at clients location, hybrid mode - On Site as well as OffShore.
